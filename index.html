What is Munshiji?

Munshiji is your one-stop solution for all financial advisory needs. Our goal is to simplify your investment journey with expert guidance, data-driven insights, and real-time portfolio management.

Investment planning platforms that could provide personalized investment strategies are not easily accessible. Also, they are quite expensive and do not cover multiple asset classes. Additionally, human intervention on such platforms adds to the expense, therefore, we attempt to automate the platform using AIOps techniques.

Making wise investment planning strategies more easily accessible to the common people in a cost-effective and secure manner by ensuring investment across diversified asset classes.
